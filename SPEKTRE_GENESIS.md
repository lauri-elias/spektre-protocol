## What Spektre-Genesis Is

Spektre-Genesis is **not** a product, a framework, or a deployment target.

It is the **genesis layer** of the Spektre ecosystem:
the point where architecture becomes executable reality.

If **Spektre Protocol v1.1** defines *how* state, responsibility, and commitment must behave,  
**Spektre-Genesis** documents *that the architecture has crossed into existence*.

Genesis is not a beginning.
It is the moment a system becomes irreversible.

---

## Purpose

Spektre-Genesis exists to answer one question only:

**“What happens after the protocol is correct?”**

The answer is not expansion.
The answer is **stability under execution**.

Genesis marks:
- The first complete state commit
- The transition from theory to lived architecture
- The point where the system no longer depends on belief, narrative, or explanation

After Genesis, the system **does not need justification**.
