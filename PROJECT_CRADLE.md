## 0. Important Framing (Read First)

**Project Cradle is not a medical claim.**  
It does not assert that aging can be reversed.

Project Cradle is a **systems-level research concept** exploring whether *age-related decline can be slowed, stabilized, or partially re-patterned* by reducing chronic biological and cognitive friction.

No miracles.  
No immortality.  
No metaphysics.

Only architecture.

---

## 1. The Core Hypothesis

> *Aging accelerates when biological systems operate under persistent misalignment.*

Misalignment sources include:
- chronic stress signaling
- circadian disruption
- identity-role overload
- unresolved prediction error
- continuous low-grade inflammation
- cognitive overcommitment

**Cradle asks:**  
What happens if these are structurally removed?

---

## 2. What “Reverse Aging Resonator” Actually Means

A **Resonator** is not a machine.  
It is a **state-stabilizing environment**.

Reverse aging here means:

- slowing epigenetic drift
- reducing allostatic load
- restoring recovery cycles
- re-enabling cellular maintenance
- preserving neuroplasticity

In other words:

> *Less damage per unit time.*

[ Environment ]
