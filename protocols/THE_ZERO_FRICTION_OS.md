## 0. What Zero Friction Means (Precisely)

**Zero Friction** does NOT mean:
- effortlessness
- comfort
- ease
- passivity

Zero Friction means:

> *No unnecessary resistance between state, intent, and action.*

Energy is still spent.  
Difficulty still exists.  
But **nothing leaks**.

---

## 1. The Problem Zero Friction OS Solves

Modern humans operate on **misaligned stacks**:

- Biological system ≠ social demands  
- Cognitive state ≠ task expectations  
- Internal truth ≠ external narrative  
- Tools ≠ agency boundaries  

This creates **chronic drag**:
- decision fatigue
- false urgency
- burnout
- identity confusion
- over-optimization

Zero Friction OS removes the drag **without increasing control**.

---

## 2. Core Principle

> *Friction is not caused by complexity —  
> it is caused by misaligned interfaces.*

Zero Friction OS does not simplify the world.  
It aligns **interfaces**.

[ Reality ]

> **Nothing executes unless state is explicit.**

- No action from emotion
- No action from narrative
- No action from pressure
- No action from prediction

Only from **declared state**.
