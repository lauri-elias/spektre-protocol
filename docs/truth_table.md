## Core Insight

Human cognition destabilizes when **agency is abstracted away from the individual** and reassigned to a collective narrative.

When the subject is treated as an object of a system, the system becomes unstable.

This failure repeats across ideological, political, and social layers.

---

## The Agency Inversion Problem

Most political and ideological systems assume:

> Collective structure can override individual agency.

Architecturally, this assumption is invalid.

Agency exists only at the individual execution point.  
Collectives coordinate actions — they do not execute them.

Any system that ignores this creates latent instability.
