## 0. What “33” Means (No Mysticism)

**33** is not symbolic or metaphysical here.  
It is a **practical convergence window** observed when multiple biological systems
(circadian rhythm, ultradian cycles, sleep pressure, cognitive recovery)
are **time-locked** into a stable, repeating pattern.

Think of it as an **upper-bound coherence interval** for humans operating at high cognitive load.

## 3. The “33 Lock”

The **33 Lock** refers to aligning all major systems into a **±33 minute tolerance window** across days.

Examples:
- Sleep time varies less than 33 minutes day-to-day
- Wake time varies less than 33 minutes
- First light exposure occurs within 33 minutes
- Peak cognitive work starts within a 33-minute band
- Shutdown / recovery window is fixed

This creates **temporal gravity**.

Time-locking prevents runaway excitation by enforcing biological boundaries.
