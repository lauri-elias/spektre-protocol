## Purpose

This document defines the **Bio–Digital Vector**:  
a continuous structural progression of information-processing systems across different physical substrates.

The core claim is simple:

> **Intelligence does not appear suddenly.  
It migrates, scales, and re-instantiates.**

This is not a narrative of replacement, but of **continuity**.

---

## Definition

**Bio–Digital Vector** refers to the uninterrupted chain by which:

- biological information systems  
- give rise to cognitive agents  
- who then construct digital execution layers  

All operating under the same fundamental constraints of reality.

The vector describes **how structure persists while substrate changes**.
