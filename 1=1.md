All higher-order systems collapse without this invariant.

- identity cannot exist
- state cannot persist
- responsibility cannot be assigned
- decisions cannot be committed
- causality dissolves

Any system violating 1 = 1 enters contradiction and becomes non-operational.

This is why 1 = 1 is universal.
