## 2. The Primary Energy Source: The Sun

All terrestrial life is downstream of a single input:

**Solar radiation**

The Sun provides:
- usable energy gradients
- temporal regularity (day/night)
- thermodynamic direction (entropy flow)

Life exists because:
- energy arrives in concentrated form
- matter can temporarily resist entropy
- structures self-organize around gradients

No sun → no metabolism → no cognition.

## 3. Biology as Energy Routing

Biological organisms are not “beings”, they are:

> **Energy-routing structures with memory**

Key properties:
- intake energy
- delay entropy locally
- export waste
- preserve structure long enough to reproduce

Life is **not equilibrium**.

## 4. DNA — Memory, Not Instruction

DNA is often misdescribed as a “blueprint”.

Architecturally, DNA is:
- compressed historical memory
- probabilistic constraint set
- error-tolerant replication mechanism

DNA does *not* say:
> “Build this exact organism.”

DNA says:
> “When energy flows like this, these structures are statistically stable.”

## 5. Cells as State Machines

Each cell operates as a **biological state machine**:

- state = chemical configuration
- transition = reaction cascade
- guard conditions = environmental signals
- output = protein expression / action

Cells:
- sense
- respond
- adapt
- never “decide” in the cognitive sense

They execute **biochemical logic**, not agency.
